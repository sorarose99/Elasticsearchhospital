## üé¨ Video Editing Tips

### For Professional Results:

1. **Transitions**: Use smooth fades and slides (0.5-1 second)
2. **Text Overlays**: Sans-serif font, large and readable
3. **Color Grading**: Consistent navy/teal theme throughout
4. **Music**: Subtle, professional background music (medical/tech genre)
5. **Sound Effects**: Subtle UI sounds for clicks and transitions
6. **Pacing**: Keep it moving, don't linger too long on any scene

### Recommended Tools:
- **DaVinci Resolve** (Free, professional)
- **Adobe Premiere Pro** (Professional)
- **Final Cut Pro** (Mac)
- **CapCut** (Easy, free)
- **Descript** (AI-powered, easy voiceover)
