## üé§ Recording Tips

### Voiceover
- **Warm up** your voice before recording
- **Speak clearly** and confidently
- **Pause** after key points
- **Emphasize** important words (faster, smarter, safer)
- **Record in quiet room** with good acoustics
- **Use quality microphone** (Blue Yeti, Rode NT-USB)

### Screen Recording
- **1920x1080** resolution minimum
- **60 fps** for smooth animations
- **Clean desktop** (no distractions)
- **Hide cursor** when not clicking
- **Practice clicks** before recording
- **Use OBS Studio** or similar

### Editing
- **Cut dead space** between scenes
- **Add transitions** (fade, slide)
- **Sync audio** perfectly with visuals
- **Add background music** (subtle)
- **Color grade** for consistency
- **Export in H.264** (MP4)
